Privacy Policy

DMV Test Prep 2026
Last updated: February 16, 2026

Brush Trading ("we", "our", "us") built DMV Test Prep 2026: Practice Exam ("the App") as a free, ad-supported application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use the App.

Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account or sign-up is required
  • No name, email, phone number, or address is collected
  • No location data is collected
  • No photos, contacts, or files are accessed
  • No data is sent to our servers — there are no servers

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Settings preferences — your selected state, theme, appearance mode, and text size
  • Practice progress — which questions you've answered, your accuracy, exam scores, and XP/level progress
  • Bookmarks — questions you've saved for later review
  • Question database — DMV and motorcycle practice questions bundled with the App covering all 50 US states
  • Daily challenges — your progress on daily challenge questions
  • Badges — achievement badges you've unlocked (Premium feature)

This data never leaves your device. If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data is deleted.

Third-Party Services

We display banner advertisements through Google AdMob to support the free availability of the App. Google AdMob may collect and use data as described in Google's privacy policy. This may include:

You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings under Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization.

Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Premium Features

The App offers optional Premium features through in-app purchase. Premium purchases are processed through Google Play's billing system, which has its own privacy policy. We do not process payments directly or store payment information.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The App contains no user-generated content, social features, or chat functionality.
